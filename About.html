Quality

Wood And String Quality

Dalbergia, also known by the names Sheesham wood and Indian Rosewood is the penultimate quality of wood bested only by the pure Brazilian Rosewood

which is a tad warmer than the former. They sound slightly different, but both just as good and although they are found in totally different geographies, there

has been no equal for the sweet sound of the Dalbergia in south-east Asia and India.

The genus Toona Ciliata, commonly known as the red cedar, is native to India and is used for high-grade export-quality musical instruments. It is considered

to be of a grade higher than its siblings such as the Toona Sinensis found in China, Toona Sureni found in Indonesia and Toona Calantis found in the

archipelago of Philippines. It is considered at par with the Cuban Cedrela Odorata and the Mexican Guarea Grandifolia, which are considered to be

paramount in quality for the manufacturing of musical instruments.

The wood used in our guitar making is derived from the direct strain that was initially planted as a plantation crop in Hawaii in 1918 using seeds from

Australia and has since become naturalized. Such quality is peerless in the entire Indian sub-continent and is sought-after by guitar enthusiasts not only

in India but worldwide.

guitar pickup

A guitar pickup device is a transducer (specifically, a variable reluctance sensor) that captures or senses mechanical vibrations produced by stringed

instruments such such as the guitar and converts them to an electrical signal that is amplified using an instrument amplifier to produce musical sounds

through a loudspeaker in a speaker enclosure. Most newbie enthusiasts are unaware that semi-acoustic does not mean that a guitar is anything less than

an acoustic one. In fact, it is simply an acoustic guitar fixed with a pickup device. Every Signature Musicals guitar comes with a passive pickup which you

can connect to either an amplifier or a speaker. We also supply active pickups on request.

This functionality is absent in copycat and fake products sold by dubious sellers.
